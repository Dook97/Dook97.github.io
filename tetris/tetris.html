about & help

Controls

Use 'A' & 'D' to move the active tetromino sideways.

Use 'W' to rotate the active tetromino clockwise.

Use 'S' to drop the active tetromino.

Use 'P' to pause/unpause the game.

Use 'R' to restart the game at any time.

Rules

The goal of the game is to stack the falling tetrominos for as long as possible and gain score in the process. The game ends when one of the pieces ends up touching the top of the playing field.

Score is gained when a row is entirely filled. This row then disappears and the above pieces 'fall' down into its place. Score is calculated using the following formula:

score gain = 10 × (2 rows deleted at once - 1)

That means you can get up to 150 score points, if you manage to remove four rows at once (the pros call such move 'tetris' apparently), or as little as ten if you only remove one.
